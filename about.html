About Vorici Calc: Passionate PoE Crafters Helping You Master Patch 3.24

Our Mission

Vorici Calc was created with one simple mission: to take the guesswork out of Path of Exile socket crafting. We believe that every player should have access to the tools and knowledge they need to optimize their gear without wasting precious currency.

Since our founding in 2022, we've been dedicated to maintaining and improving our calculator with every new Path of Exile patch. Our latest update for Patch 3.24 includes revised crafting costs, updated socket coloring probabilities, and new features to help you plan your crafting strategy.

The Story Behind Vorici Calc

The idea for Vorici Calc began when our founder, a long-time Path of Exile player, spent over 1,500 Chromatic Orbs trying to get 4 blue sockets on a Str/Dex hybrid chest. After that frustrating experience, he realized there had to be a better way.

By combining data mining, community research, and extensive testing, we developed the first version of our calculator. What started as a simple spreadsheet has evolved into the comprehensive tool you see today, used by thousands of players to optimize their crafting strategies.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Updating our calculator with each new Path of Exile patch
  • Providing accurate, data-driven recommendations
  • Creating helpful guides and resources for the community
  • Maintaining a tool that's free and accessible to all players

Whether you're a seasoned crafter or new to Path of Exile, we're here to help you save time, currency, and frustration. Thank you for using Vorici Calc!
