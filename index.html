Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Vorici Calculator

How does the Vorici Calculator determine the best crafting method?

Our calculator uses advanced algorithms that analyze multiple factors including:

  • Item type and base attributes
  • Required attribute values (Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence)
  • Number of desired sockets and their colors
  • Current game mechanics and probabilities from Patch 3.24
It then calculates the most cost-effective method, whether that's using Chromatic Orbs, the crafting bench, or a combination of both.

Is this calculator up to date for the latest Path of Exile patch?

Yes! Our calculator is currently updated for Path of Exile Patch 3.24. We actively maintain this tool and update it whenever there are changes to game mechanics that affect socket coloring. You can always check the version information in the footer of the page.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculations are based on extensive testing and community-verified data. While we strive for 100% accuracy, there might be slight variations due to:

  • Undocumented changes in game patches
  • Random number generation variations
  • Specific item interactions
We regularly update our algorithms based on community feedback and new discoveries.

Does quality affect socket coloring?

Yes! Quality can significantly impact your chances when using Chromatic Orbs. Here's how it works:

  • Higher quality (up to 20%) increases the chance of getting off-colors
  • Quality has no effect when using the Vorici crafting bench
  • We recommend using 20% quality before attempting to color your item
  • Armourer's Scraps can be used to increase quality
Our calculator automatically takes quality into account when calculating the optimal method.

What's the best way to get off-colors on high attribute requirements?

For items with high attribute requirements, consider these strategies:

  • Vorici Crafting Bench: Most reliable for specific color combinations
  • Chromatic Orbs with Quality: Use 20% quality before attempting
  • Jeweler's Method: Sometimes more efficient for multiple off-colors
  • Attribute Adjustment: Lowering attributes can help (temporarily remove gear with +attributes)
Our calculator will recommend the most efficient method based on your specific item and desired colors.
