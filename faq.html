Frequently Asked Questions About Vorici Crafting (PoE Patch 3.24)

Find answers to the most common questions about using the Vorici Calculator and crafting off-color sockets in Path of Exile.

What is Vorici's Bench in Patch 3.24?

In Path of Exile Patch 3.24, Vorici's Bench refers to a crafting station that allows players to modify socket colors on their gear. Named after Vorici, a former master from older PoE versions, this method is now accessed through the main crafting bench in your hideout.

The Vorici method offers deterministic socket coloring with fixed costs, unlike the random outcomes of Chromatic Orbs. This is especially valuable for off-color sockets (colors that don't align with the item's attribute requirements).

How do Chromatic Orbs work in the latest PoE patch?

In Path of Exile Patch 3.24, Chromatic Orbs randomize all socket colors on an item. The probability of getting specific colors is influenced by the item's attribute requirements:

  • Higher Strength requirements favor Red sockets
  • Higher Dexterity requirements favor Green sockets
  • Higher Intelligence requirements favor Blue sockets

The exact probabilities are calculated based on the attribute requirements. Items with high requirements in a single attribute are very likely to roll that color, making off-colors difficult to obtain through Chromatic Orbs alone.

What are off-color sockets and how are they affected by recent changes?

Off-color sockets are socket colors that don't match the primary attribute of an item. For example:

  • Blue sockets on a high-Strength (red) item
  • Red sockets on a high-Intelligence (blue) item
  • Green sockets on a high-Intelligence (blue) item

In Patch 3.24, the base probabilities haven't changed significantly, but crafting bench options have been adjusted for better deterministic outcomes. The Vorici calculator accounts for these changes when recommending the most efficient crafting method.

Getting multiple off-colors through Chromatic Orbs can be extremely expensive, which is why using targeted crafting methods becomes increasingly important.

What's the best way to craft RGB sockets in Patch 3.24?

The best approach for RGB sockets in Patch 3.24 depends on your item:

  • For low attribute requirement items: Direct Chromatic Orb usage is often sufficient, as these items have relatively balanced color probabilities.
  • For high attribute requirement items: The Vorici bench offers crafts specifically for tricolor sockets, which will guarantee at least one of each color.
  • For 6-socket items: Consider using the "at least X" socket crafting method, which guarantees a minimum number of a specific color while leaving the rest random.

Our calculator will analyze your specific item requirements and recommend the most efficient method based on current crafting costs.

For many builds, having a proper RGB setup is crucial for optimal gem links, especially for items that naturally favor a single color due to high attribute requirements.

How does item quality affect socket coloring?

Item quality doesn't directly affect socket coloring probabilities in Path of Exile. Quality affects other aspects of an item:

  • Weapons: Increases physical damage
  • Armor: Increases armor, evasion, or energy shield values
  • Flasks: Increases effect duration

The only factors that influence socket color probabilities are:

  • The item's Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence requirements
  • The specific crafting method used (Chromatic Orbs, crafting bench, etc.)

It's always advisable to check your item's attribute requirements before attempting to color sockets, as this will determine how difficult it will be to obtain your desired colors.

Can you still use Vorici in the Syndicate for white sockets?

Yes, in Patch 3.24, Vorici in the Immortal Syndicate can still create white sockets when placed in the Research safehouse. When you run a Research safehouse with Vorici as a member:

  • Rank 1: Vorici adds 1-2 white sockets to an item
  • Rank 2: Vorici adds 1-3 white sockets to an item
  • Rank 3: Vorici adds 1-4 white sockets to an item

White sockets can accept gems of any color, making them extremely valuable for gear flexibility. This method is one of the few ways to obtain white sockets in the game, and the outcome is random (both the number of sockets and which sockets are changed).

Our Vorici Calculator doesn't directly account for white sockets, but they're an excellent option if you need maximum flexibility in your socket colors.
