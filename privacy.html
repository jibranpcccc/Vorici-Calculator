Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2025

Introduction

At Vorici Calculator, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics through standard analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your calculator settings and preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website and calculator functionality
  • Save your calculator preferences for future visits
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize user experience

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no Internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as Google Analytics. These services may collect information about your visit to our website. Each third-party service provider has its own privacy policy.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Clear your browser's local storage
  • Use browser extensions that block tracking

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with a new effective date.
